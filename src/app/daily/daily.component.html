Daily Classes
Daily classes are for ninety minute/ plus. They include warm-ups, breathing exercises (pranayama), asanas and deep relaxation. The focus is in on strengthening the body, and also making it more flexible. It helps to improve health. The class is a combination of dynamic movement with emphasis on breathing. Helps to open the body from within. It is an energy based yoga, and not a forceful at all, but one that works according to the capacity and capability of one self.

A Perfect class for Beginners, Intermediates and Advanced.

  • Meditation

    Meditation is that natural state of the mind when it is free from anxiety and stress. Only when our mind is calm and relaxed, are we able to centre within ourselves and can take good decisions and work for our highest good.

    Meditations is mainly two kinds : We have active meditations, where you are consciously using mantras and staying in a pose to draw in a new energy, and we have passive meditations, where you just sit in silence or just be. Both are powerful, effective and healing. Meditations have the power to not just calm the mind, but also change it. And, if you want to change your life, you need to change your mind. The power lies in the mind, and we just need to elevate it.
