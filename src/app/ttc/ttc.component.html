Yoga Teacher Training Course
At Satguru Yoga we offer a traditional style of Yoga Teacher trainings. Our training depends on established teaching styles, which include: Asana, Pranayama, Meditation, Yoga Philosophy, Detoxification methods and Stress management, through teachers who have taken the time and trouble to create a deep self practice and use the wisdom of Yoga to help themselves grow.Satguru Yoga welcomes everybody to experience yoga by understanding its basic, all encompassing nature.

It is our dream of training extremely competent teachers, with a profound and deep knowledge of yoga. The chief motto of Satguru Yoga is to create skilled and compassionate yoga teachers that can spread the holistic benefits of yoga, and create a better humane society.
We'll be imparting knowledge for 200 Hour Yoga Alliance Teacher Training courses from our Residential Centre in Bhakti kutir, South Goa, India. Our 200 hours Teacher Training courses are Yoga Alliance registered, one-month intensive training certification courses that offer students the chance to get a valid and conventional teachings from our expert Indian teachers.

The syllabus of the course has been designed to understand Yoga from a basic level to a step by step build up with a complete curriculum divided into 4 modules for 4 weeks and each module will be complementary to its successor. Students will be given exposure to the yogic discipline in a conducive and positive attitude so as to prevent any misconceptions or impressions to be formed.

A strong focus will be on the therapeutic effects of asanas, poses, breathing exercises, and how to gain health with the practice of Yoga

  • Practical Sessions

    Practice and Understanding of Asanas, their Benefits, therapeutic effects, and method. Deep breathing Exercises, with emphasis on how to breathe properly, Awareness of their therapeutic effects, and how to combine right breathing with asanas and movement. Meditation, the practice and its effects. Kundalini Yoga Meditations, and understanding mantras, and their meanings. We'd go through with various disciplines and subcategories which are -

    • Raja Yoga
    • Karma Yoga
    • Bhakti Yoga
    • Mantra Yoga
    • Pratyahar


  • Theoretical Sessions

    We'd also focus on various theoretical aspects of Yogic disciplines which again have a lot of sub parts and would be covered in class both from a student's perspective and as a teacher training course perspective. We also get you through the qualities and ethics of a Yoga teacher, constructive skills on how to conduct a class, teacher manuals and explanantion of the principles andthe methods along with their advantages.
